Privacy Policy

AppLedScroller

Last updated: 2026-03-28

Privacy Summary

LedScroller is designed to be offline and privacy-focused. We do not collect personal information, do not track user behavior, and your creations are stored locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information. LedScroller does not require personal data to function.

  • No personal information
  • No usage analytics
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No contact information

2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use personal information. Any content you create (such as LED text, templates, or settings) is used only within the app on your device.

3. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using iOS storage mechanisms:

  • Preferences may be stored using UserDefaults
  • Your creations are stored locally on your device
  • No data is sent to our servers
  • Data is deleted when you uninstall the app

4. Third-Party Services

LedScroller uses minimal third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: for app distribution
  • Apple StoreKit: for in-app purchases and subscriptions (handled by Apple)

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

5. Children's Privacy

LedScroller does not collect personal information from any users, including children under 13.

6. Data Security

Because your data stays on your device, your privacy is inherently protected by iOS security features. We do not transmit your creations to external servers.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no account data for us to provide, correct, or delete. You control your local data:

  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Use iOS settings to manage app storage if applicable

8. International Users

LedScroller works the same way worldwide. We do not collect personal information regardless of your location.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
